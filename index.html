Mexican Food

On this website you will see the many types of dishes that are served in Mexico, or at Mexican restaurants

Burritos

A burrito is a collecion of ingredientes wrapped in a tortilla.

Ingredients that are usually put in burritos are...
Rice
Cheese(Shredded)
Beans(Black, Pinto, etc.)
Salsa or Guacamole
Lettuce(Preferred type is iceburg lettuce)
Chicken(Or beef)
Sour Cream
And much more!

Nachos

Nachos are a variety of toppings all on top of a plate of tortilla chips.

Ingredients most commonly used in nachos are...
Cheese
Salsa
Guacamole
Beans

Fun Fact: National Nacho Day is November 6th!

Tacos

A taco, like a burrito, is a collection of ingredients in a tortilla. Unlike burritos, tacos use shells to hold the food and are only halfway open instead of being closed. Most taco shells are hard, although they can be made soft (known as soft tacos).

Ingredients that are popular in tacos are...
Rice
Beans
Shredded Cheese
Salsa
Chicken/Beef

Churros

A churro is a fried-dough pastry that is served hot. They're mostly eaten as a dessert or snack, but they can also be made for a main meal.

Churros are usually coted with some sugar when served. They're also often dipped in hot chocolate, coffee, or champurrado (a chocolate-based drink). Churros are filled with cajeta, which is a sweet syrup made of chocolate and goat's milk.

Enchiladas

An enchilada is a corn tortilla rolled around a filling and covered in chili sauce. Enchiladas can be filled with many other ingredients.

Fillings include meat (For example; beef, poultry, pork, seafood) or cheese, potatoes, vegetables, and any combination of these. Enchiladas are commonly topped or garnished with cheese, sour cream, lettuce, olives, chopped onions, chili peppers, salsa, or fresh cilantro.

Fun Fact: Enchiladas date back to the Mayans,
who are believed to be the creators of the dish!
